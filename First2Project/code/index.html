Who we are

SKP Company, formed by two industry experts, is named after Skopje airport code. We specialize in international transport, logistics, and forwardin, focusing on customs representation for import-export processes. Our main activities involve facilitating communication with state services, obtaining permits, and handling documentation for shipped goods.
